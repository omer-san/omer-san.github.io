This was my first time in Japan, and I wanted the must-see attractions and also something extra, to experience the culture. Omer planned each day for me with a lot of options in each area to choose from. He picked things that are close to each other, so it was very organized and convenient. 

I saw fall leaves for the first time in the alps, and loved the art in Naoshima. These were the highlights for me, because they gave me that something extra. He included instructions for public transportation in the countryside, and even reserved a bike for me in Naoshima. 

I had a whole day planned for Mount Fuji, but the weather was bad, so he changed it for Hakone at the last minute. I enjoyed the traditional vibe at an old teahouse that served a rice milk drink, and also tried a hot spring. He booked a tour of the volcano with a guide, but I thought that was unnecessary. 

Unfortunately, I didn’t like the food at the ryokan in Miyajima. And it was hard to carry my suitcase to a different place every day. But now I know what works for me and what doesn’t for the next time.
